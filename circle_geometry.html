Circle Geometry

A circle is a shape containing a set of points that are all the same distance from its center.

A circle has some important dimensions: the radius, the diameter and the circumference. It also has an area, which is the amount of space contained by a circle.

This widget allows you to find the radius, diameter, circumference and area of a circle.
